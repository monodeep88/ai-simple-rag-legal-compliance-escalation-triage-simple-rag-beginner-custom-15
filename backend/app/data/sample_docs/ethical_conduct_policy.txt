This Ethical Conduct Policy outlines the standards of behavior expected from all employees, contractors, and partners. Key principles include integrity, respect, fairness, and accountability. Employees must avoid conflicts of interest, bribery, and corruption. Gifts of nominal value are permitted, but anything exceeding $50 requires manager approval. Any situations that appear to be a conflict of interest must be disclosed to your manager and the HR department. Failure to adhere to this policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination.
