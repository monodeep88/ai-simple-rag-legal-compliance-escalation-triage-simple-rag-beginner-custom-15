Serious Compliance Incident Reporting Guidelines:
1.  **Immediate Notification (within 24 hours):** Any incident that could lead to significant legal exposure, financial loss, or reputational damage must be reported to the Head of Compliance and Legal Department immediately (within 24 business hours).
2.  **Documentation:** Complete an 'Incident Report Form' detailing the nature of the incident, individuals involved, date, time, and any initial actions taken.
3.  **Severity Assessment:** The Compliance Department will assess the severity (Low, Medium, High) and potential impact.
4.  **Escalation Matrix:** High severity incidents are automatically escalated to the Executive Leadership Team. Medium incidents require a remediation plan within 7 days. Low incidents are resolved by the local management with oversight.
5.  **Data Breaches:** Suspected data breaches involving personal identifiable information (PII) must also be reported to the IT Security Team immediately.
